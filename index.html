Comprehensive Analysis

Why Gut Health Testing?

Comprehensive stool analysis—using quantitative PCR technology to measure 40+ markers—reveals what a standard GI appointment cannot. It is the difference between guessing and knowing.

Microbiome Balance

Ratios of beneficial to pathogenic bacteria, overgrowth patterns, and keystone species that determine how well your gut protects and communicates.

Inflammation Markers

Calprotectin and secretory IgA levels identify immune activation and mucosal integrity—early signals that standard blood work misses entirely.

Digestive Function

Pancreatic elastase, fat absorption, and short-chain fatty acid production indicate how efficiently your body is actually using what you eat.

Gut-Brain Connection

Dysbiosis is directly linked to anxiety, depression, and cognitive fog through the gut-brain axis. Restoring gut health often resolves symptoms that seem unrelated.

GI-MAP testing uses quantitative PCR (qPCR) technology. Testing coordinated through our lab partners with physician sign-off — at-home collection kits shipped directly to you, results interpreted as part of your personalized wellness protocol.
